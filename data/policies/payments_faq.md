# ShopNova Payments & Cancellations FAQ

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2024
**Document Version:** 4.0

---

## 1. Overview

This document covers all questions related to payments, billing, order cancellations, and refund processing on the ShopNova platform. ShopNova supports multiple payment methods to accommodate the preferences of customers across Brazil, and this FAQ is designed to answer the most common questions about each method clearly and completely.

For questions not covered here, customers may contact ShopNova support through the app, website chat, or by email at suporte@shopnova.com.br.

---

## 2. Accepted Payment Methods

ShopNova accepts the following payment methods for all orders placed on the platform:

### 2.1 Credit Card

Credit cards are the most widely used payment method on ShopNova and offer the most flexibility including instalment payments. ShopNova accepts all major credit card brands operating in Brazil:

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Elo
- Hipercard

Credit card payments are processed in real time at checkout. The charge will appear on the customer's statement under the name "SHOPNOVA" or "SHOPNOVA*SELLER" depending on the card issuer. ShopNova uses 3DS2 (3D Secure) authentication for added payment security on all credit card transactions.

### 2.2 Debit Card

Debit cards are accepted from all major Brazilian banks. Debit card payments are processed in real time and the amount is deducted immediately from the customer's account. Instalment payments are not available for debit card purchases — the full amount is charged at checkout.

Debit card payments require the cardholder to authenticate the transaction through their bank's app or SMS OTP. If authentication fails, the order will not be confirmed.

### 2.3 Boleto Bancário

Boleto is a Brazilian payment method that generates a payment slip (boleto) that can be paid at any bank branch, ATM, internet banking portal, or lottery house (casa lotérica) in Brazil.

Key boleto rules:

- A boleto generated at checkout must be paid within **3 business days**. If not paid within this window, the boleto expires and the order is automatically cancelled.
- Once paid, boleto payment confirmation takes **1–2 business days** to be processed by the banking system. The order will only be dispatched after payment is confirmed.
- Boleto payments cannot be split into instalments.
- Boleto refunds cannot be reversed to the original boleto. Refunds for boleto payments are issued via bank transfer (TED) to the customer's nominated account (see Section 6.2).

### 2.4 ShopNova Voucher

ShopNova vouchers are store credit codes issued as refunds, promotional rewards, or gifts. Vouchers can be applied at checkout by entering the voucher code in the "Discount/Voucher" field.

- Vouchers have no expiry date unless specified on the voucher itself.
- Multiple vouchers can be applied to a single order, up to a maximum of 3 vouchers per transaction.
- Vouchers cannot be combined with certain promotional offers — restrictions will be displayed at checkout.
- Vouchers cannot be exchanged for cash.
- If the order total exceeds the voucher value, the remainder can be paid with any other accepted payment method.

## 3. Instalment Payment Rules

### 3.1 Availability

Instalment payments (parcelamento) are available exclusively for credit card purchases. The number of instalments available depends on the order total:

| Order total | Maximum instalments |
|---|---|
| R$50 – R$99 | 2x |
| R$100 – R$199 | 3x |
| R$200 – R$499 | 6x |
| R$500 – R$999 | 9x |
| R$1,000 and above | 12x |

### 3.2 Interest-Free Instalments

ShopNova offers **interest-free instalments** on orders above R$100 for up to 6 instalments. No additional fees are charged for interest-free instalments — the total order value is simply divided equally across the chosen number of instalments.

For instalments above 6x (available on orders of R$500+), a monthly interest rate applies. The applicable rate and total amount payable will be displayed clearly at checkout before the customer confirms the order. The interest rate varies between 1.5% and 2.5% per month depending on the customer's card issuer.

### 3.3 How Instalments Work

The first instalment is charged immediately at the time of purchase. Subsequent instalments are charged monthly on the same day of the month as the original purchase. All instalments are charged to the same credit card used at checkout.

If a customer wishes to pay off remaining instalments early, they should contact their credit card issuer directly, as this is handled by the card network and not by ShopNova.

### 3.4 Instalment Refunds

If an order paid in instalments is refunded, the refund is processed as a single lump sum to the credit card, not as a reversal of individual instalments. The credit card issuer then distributes the refund against the outstanding instalments. This process may take up to 2 billing cycles to be fully reflected on the customer's statement.

## 4. Boleto — Detailed Rules

### 4.1 Generating a Boleto

A boleto is generated automatically at the end of checkout when the customer selects boleto as their payment method. The boleto is displayed on screen and sent to the customer's registered email address. It can also be retrieved at any time from "My Orders" → "View Boleto."

### 4.2 Boleto Payment Window

The boleto must be paid within **3 business days** of the order being placed. If the 3-day window expires over a weekend or national holiday, the deadline is extended to the next business day.

### 4.3 Expired Boleto

If the boleto expires unpaid, the order is automatically cancelled and no charge is made. The customer may place the order again, which will generate a new boleto. ShopNova cannot reactivate an expired boleto — a new order must be placed.

### 4.4 Boleto Payment Confirmation Delay

After paying a boleto, the banking system takes between **1 and 2 business days** to confirm the payment to ShopNova. During this time, the order status will show "Awaiting payment confirmation." Once confirmed, the status will update to "Payment confirmed" and the seller will be notified to dispatch.

### 4.5 Boleto Reissue

If a customer loses the boleto or it has expired, they may request a reissue by going to "My Orders" → "Reissue Boleto." The reissued boleto will have a new 3-day payment window. Boleto reissue is only available for orders that have not yet been cancelled.

## 5. Payment Failures and Retries

### 5.1 Card Declined at Checkout

If a credit or debit card is declined at checkout, the order will not be confirmed. Common reasons for card declines include:

- Insufficient funds or credit limit
- Card blocked for online transactions
- 3D Secure authentication failure
- Incorrect card details entered
- Card issuer fraud prevention block

The customer should check with their card issuer to understand the reason for the decline. ShopNova does not have visibility into the specific reason for a card decline.

### 5.2 Order Hold Period

When a payment fails after checkout (for example, an instalment that fails to charge on the due date), ShopNova places the order on hold for **48 hours**. The customer will receive an email and in-app notification with instructions to update their payment method.

If the payment is not resolved within 48 hours, the order is cancelled. Any items that were reserved for the order will be released back to inventory.

### 5.3 Updating Payment Information

To update the payment method for an order on hold:

1. Go to "My Orders" and find the order with "Payment issue" status.
2. Click "Update Payment."
3. Enter new card details or select a different payment method.
4. Confirm the updated payment.

If the updated payment is approved, the order will resume from where it was in the fulfilment process.

## 6. Order Cancellation

### 6.1 Free Cancellation Window

Customers may cancel an order **free of charge within 1 hour** of placing it, regardless of the payment method used. This window exists to allow customers to correct mistakes made at checkout (wrong size, wrong colour, wrong address).

To cancel within the 1-hour window:

1. Go to "My Orders" immediately after placing the order.
2. Select the order and click "Cancel Order."
3. Choose a cancellation reason.
4. Confirm. A cancellation confirmation will be sent by email.

### 6.2 Cancellation After 1 Hour — Before Dispatch

After the 1-hour free cancellation window but before the seller has dispatched the order, customers may still request a cancellation. ShopNova will contact the seller to attempt to halt the order. Cancellation is not guaranteed at this stage and depends on the seller's fulfilment speed.

If the seller has already prepared the order for dispatch, the cancellation may not be possible. In this case, the customer must wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

### 6.3 Cancellation After Dispatch

Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible. The customer must wait to receive the item and then submit a return request through "My Orders" following the Return & Refund Policy.

### 6.4 Seller-Initiated Cancellations

In some cases, a seller may cancel an order due to stock unavailability, pricing errors, or inability to fulfil. If a seller cancels an order, the customer will be notified by email and will receive an automatic full refund within 3 business days. Customers are also entitled to a R$15 inconvenience credit applied to their ShopNova account.

## 7. Refund Processing by Payment Method

### 7.1 Credit Card Refunds

Refunds to credit cards are processed by ShopNova within **5–7 business days** of the refund being approved. The time for the credit to appear on the customer's statement depends on their card issuer's billing cycle and may take an additional 1–3 business days. Total time from refund approval to credit appearing: typically **6–10 business days**.

Instalment refunds: if the order was paid in instalments, the full refund is applied as a single credit to the card. The card issuer distributes this against outstanding instalments. This may take up to **2 billing cycles** to be fully reflected.

### 7.2 Boleto Refunds

Boleto refunds are processed as bank transfers (TED/DOC) since boleto payments cannot be reversed. To receive a boleto refund, the customer must:

1. Provide their bank details through the ShopNova refund portal (accessible from the return confirmation email).
2. Required details: bank name, branch number (agência), account number (conta), account type (corrente or poupança), and CPF.

Boleto refunds are processed within **10 business days** of the customer providing their bank details. ShopNova will send a payment confirmation email once the transfer is complete.

### 7.3 Debit Card Refunds

Debit card refunds follow the same process as credit card refunds. The amount is returned to the original debit card within **5–7 business days**. Processing times depend on the customer's bank.

### 7.4 Voucher Refunds

If the original payment was made entirely or partially with a ShopNova voucher:

- The voucher portion of the refund is re-issued as a new voucher code within **24 hours** of the refund being approved.
- The new voucher has the same value as the refunded amount and has no expiry date.
- If a combination of voucher and card was used, the voucher portion is refunded as a new voucher and the card portion is refunded to the original card.

## 8. Promo Codes and Vouchers

### 8.1 Applying a Promo Code

Promo codes are applied at checkout in the "Promo Code" field. Only one promo code can be applied per order. Promo codes cannot be combined with other promo codes but can be used alongside ShopNova vouchers (store credit).

### 8.2 Promo Code Stacking Rules

- Maximum 1 promo code per order.
- Maximum 3 voucher codes per order.
- A promo code and up to 3 vouchers may be used on the same order.
- Promo codes cannot be applied to orders already discounted through flash sales or bundle offers, unless the promotion explicitly states otherwise.

### 8.3 Promo Code Expiry

All promo codes have an expiry date displayed in the promotional communication. Expired promo codes cannot be reactivated. ShopNova support cannot extend promo code validity.

### 8.4 Vouchers on Returned Orders

If a promo code or voucher was used to purchase an item that is later returned:

- **Promo code**: The discount value is not refunded. The customer receives a refund for the amount actually paid, not the original price.
- **Voucher (store credit)**: The voucher amount used is re-issued as a new voucher of equal value.

## 9. Fraud and Chargebacks

### 9.1 Unauthorised Charges

If a customer notices a charge on their card or bank statement from ShopNova that they did not authorise, they should:

1. Check their ShopNova order history to confirm whether the charge matches a recent order.
2. If no matching order is found, contact ShopNova support immediately at suporte@shopnova.com.br with the transaction date and amount.
3. ShopNova will investigate within 2 business days and reverse any confirmed unauthorised charge.

### 9.2 Chargeback Process

If a customer has already filed a chargeback with their card issuer, ShopNova's fraud team will be notified and will respond to the card issuer's investigation. Customers who file a chargeback for a legitimate order may have their ShopNova account suspended pending investigation.

ShopNova encourages customers to contact ShopNova support before filing a chargeback, as internal resolution is typically faster (2–5 business days) than the card network chargeback process (30–90 days).

### 9.3 Account Security

If a customer suspects their ShopNova account has been compromised, they should:

1. Change their ShopNova password immediately.
2. Review recent orders for any unrecognised purchases.
3. Contact ShopNova support to flag the account for review.
4. Report to their bank if payment credentials may have been exposed.

ShopNova uses industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) and never stores full card numbers on its servers. All payment data is handled by PCI-DSS certified payment processors.
