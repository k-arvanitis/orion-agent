# ShopNova Return & Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2024
**Document Version:** 3.2

---

## 1. Overview and Scope

ShopNova is committed to ensuring every customer is fully satisfied with their purchase. This Return & Refund Policy outlines the conditions, timeframes, and procedures that apply to all purchases made through the ShopNova marketplace. This policy applies to all orders placed by individual consumers on the ShopNova platform, regardless of the payment method used or the seller fulfilling the order.

ShopNova operates as a marketplace connecting buyers with third-party sellers. While sellers are responsible for fulfilling orders, ShopNova guarantees the enforcement of this policy across all transactions. If a seller fails to honour a valid return request, ShopNova will intervene directly to ensure the customer receives the appropriate resolution.

This policy is governed by Brazilian consumer protection law (Código de Defesa do Consumidor — CDC) and applicable federal regulations. Customers retain all statutory rights provided by law, which this policy supplements but never diminishes.

### 1.1 Definitions

- **Return window**: The period during which a customer may initiate a return, counted from the confirmed delivery date.
- **Delivery date**: The date recorded in ShopNova's system as the date the package was received by the customer, as confirmed by the carrier's tracking update.
- **Seller**: The third-party merchant who listed and fulfilled the product on ShopNova.
- **Refund**: A reimbursement to the customer's original payment method or as store credit, at the customer's discretion.
- **Exchange**: Replacement of the original item with an identical or equivalent item.
- **Defective item**: A product that does not function as described due to a manufacturing fault, not due to customer misuse or accidental damage.

## 2. General Return Window

### 2.1 Standard Return Period

Customers may return most items within **30 calendar days** of the confirmed delivery date. The return window begins on the day the carrier marks the package as delivered, regardless of whether the customer was present to receive it.

To be eligible for a standard return, the following conditions must be met:

- The item must be in its original condition, unused and unaltered.
- All original packaging, tags, accessories, and documentation must be included.
- The customer must provide proof of purchase (order ID or order confirmation email).
- The return must be initiated through the ShopNova platform before the window expires.

### 2.2 How to Count the Return Window

The 30-day window is counted in calendar days, not business days. Day 1 is the day after the confirmed delivery date. For example, if an order is delivered on January 10, the return window closes at midnight on February 9.

If the last day of the return window falls on a national public holiday in Brazil, the deadline is extended to the next business day.

### 2.3 Proof of Purchase Requirements

Customers must provide at least one of the following to initiate a return:

- ShopNova order ID (format: alphanumeric, found in order confirmation email)
- Screenshot of the order confirmation page
- Physical receipt if provided by the seller

Returns without proof of purchase will not be processed. In exceptional circumstances where proof has been lost, customers may contact ShopNova support with account login credentials as an alternative verification method.

## 3. Category-Specific Return Rules

Different product categories have specific return conditions that override the general 30-day rule where applicable. Customers should check the category-specific rule for their item before initiating a return.

### 3.1 Electronics

Electronics include smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, cameras, headphones, speakers, smart home devices, and any device with electronic components.

- **Sealed / unopened**: 30-day return window. Full refund.
- **Opened**: 7-day return window from delivery date. Item must be in full working condition with all original accessories, cables, manuals, and packaging.
- **Defective**: 90-day window (see Section 5 — Defective Items).

Electronics returned opened after the 7-day window but within 30 days will only be accepted if a manufacturing defect is demonstrated. Cosmetic damage caused by the customer (scratches, cracks, liquid damage) will void the return eligibility.

### 3.2 Clothing and Footwear

Clothing and footwear may be returned within **30 days** of delivery under the following conditions:

- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and unaltered.
- Original tags must still be attached.
- Items must be free from odours, stains, or signs of use.
- Shoes must be returned in their original box, which must be undamaged.

ShopNova offers **free return shipping** on all clothing and footwear returns. A prepaid return label will be generated automatically when the return is approved.

Swimwear and underwear may only be returned if still sealed in original hygiene packaging. Once the hygiene seal is broken, these items cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

### 3.3 Furniture and Home Décor

Furniture and large home décor items may be returned within **30 days** of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

- Items must be unassembled and in original packaging. Once assembled, furniture cannot be returned unless defective.
- Customers are responsible for disassembling and repackaging the item for return.
- Return shipping for furniture is at the customer's expense due to size and weight considerations. ShopNova will provide a subsidised carrier quote upon request.
- Custom-made or made-to-order furniture cannot be returned unless defective.

### 3.4 Beauty and Personal Care

Beauty products, skincare, cosmetics, and personal care items may be returned within **30 days** if:

- The product is sealed and unopened.
- The product has not been used or tested.

Once opened, beauty and personal care products cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons, unless the product caused an adverse reaction. In the case of an adverse reaction, the customer must provide a description of the reaction and, where possible, medical documentation. ShopNova will review these cases individually.

### 3.5 Sports and Leisure Equipment

Sports equipment, gym accessories, bicycles, and outdoor gear may be returned within **30 days** provided:

- Items are in original, unused condition with all tags and packaging intact.
- Bicycles and large equipment must be unassembled and in original packaging.
- Protective gear (helmets, knee pads, etc.) cannot be returned once used for safety reasons.

### 3.6 Home Appliances

Home appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, microwaves, air conditioners, etc.) may be returned within **30 days** if:

- The appliance is unused and in original packaging.
- Once installed or used, appliances cannot be returned unless defective (see Section 5).
- Return logistics for large appliances will be coordinated by ShopNova's delivery partner. Customers must schedule a pickup through the platform.

### 3.7 Books, Stationery, and Office Supplies

Books and stationery may be returned within **30 days** if:

- Books are in original condition, unread, and without any annotations, highlighting, or damage.
- Stationery and office supplies are unopened and in original packaging.
- Digital or downloadable content associated with a physical book cannot be refunded once accessed.

## 4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances, except where a manufacturing defect is present:

### 4.1 Perishable Goods

Food items, fresh produce, frozen goods, and any product with an expiry date of less than 60 days from the purchase date are non-returnable. If a perishable item arrives spoiled or damaged, the customer must report this within **24 hours** of delivery with photographic evidence to receive a full refund.

### 4.2 Digital Products and Downloads

Software licences, digital games, e-books, streaming subscriptions, and any other digital product that has been accessed, downloaded, or activated cannot be returned or refunded. Before purchasing digital products, customers are encouraged to review product descriptions and system requirements carefully.

### 4.3 Personalised and Custom-Made Items

Items that have been personalised, engraved, embroidered, or custom-manufactured to the customer's specification cannot be returned unless they arrive defective or do not match the approved specification provided at the time of order.

### 4.4 Hazardous Materials

Aerosols, flammable liquids, chemical cleaning agents, batteries, and any product classified as hazardous cannot be returned by post. Customers who receive a defective hazardous product should contact ShopNova support for disposal and refund instructions.

### 4.5 Undergarments and Swimwear

Underwear, bras, bikinis, and swimwear are non-returnable once the hygiene seal or original packaging has been opened, for health and safety reasons.

## 5. Defective and Damaged Items

### 5.1 Manufacturing Defects

If a product develops a fault due to a manufacturing defect, it may be returned within **90 days** of delivery, regardless of the category-specific return window. A defect is defined as a fault that is present in the product at the time of manufacture and is not caused by the customer's use, misuse, or accidental damage.

To report a defective item:

1. Navigate to "My Orders" in the ShopNova app or website.
2. Select the relevant order and click "Report a Problem."
3. Choose "Defective Item" and provide a written description of the fault.
4. Upload at least two clear photographs showing the defect.
5. Submit the report. A support agent will respond within 2 business days.

### 5.2 Items Damaged on Arrival

If an item arrives visibly damaged (broken packaging, crushed box, cracked product), the customer must report this within **48 hours** of the confirmed delivery date. Reports submitted after 48 hours may not be eligible for an immediate replacement.

To report damage on arrival:

1. Photograph the damaged packaging and product before opening further.
2. Submit a report through "My Orders" → "Report a Problem" → "Damaged on Arrival."
3. Include at least three photographs: outer packaging, inner packaging, and the damaged item itself.

ShopNova will arrange a replacement shipment or full refund within 3 business days of report verification.

### 5.3 Exceptions for Defective Returns

The following will void a defective item return claim:

- Physical damage caused by the customer (drops, cracks, liquid exposure).
- Damage resulting from use contrary to the product's intended purpose.
- Modifications or repairs attempted by the customer or an unauthorised third party.
- Normal wear and tear consistent with the product's age and usage.

## 6. Return Process — Step by Step

### 6.1 Initiating a Return

1. Log in to your ShopNova account.
2. Go to "My Orders" and locate the order containing the item you wish to return.
3. Click "Return Item" next to the relevant product.
4. Select the reason for return from the dropdown menu.
5. Choose your preferred resolution: refund to original payment method, store credit, or exchange.
6. Submit the return request. You will receive a confirmation email within 1 hour.

### 6.2 Packaging Requirements

Items must be securely packaged to prevent damage during transit. ShopNova recommends:

- Using the original packaging where possible.
- Wrapping fragile items in bubble wrap or similar protective material.
- Sealing the box with strong packing tape on all edges.
- Placing the return authorisation slip (sent by email) inside the package.

ShopNova is not responsible for items damaged during return transit due to inadequate packaging.

### 6.3 Drop-off vs Pickup

Customers may return items via two methods:

- **Drop-off**: Bring the packaged item to any authorised ShopNova return point or partner carrier location. A list of nearby drop-off points is displayed in the return confirmation email.
- **Scheduled pickup**: For large items or customers unable to visit a drop-off point, a free pickup can be scheduled through the platform. Pickup is available Monday to Saturday, 8am–6pm, subject to availability in the customer's region.

### 6.4 Tracking the Return

Once the return is dropped off or collected, a tracking number will be provided via email and displayed in "My Orders." The return status will update as the item moves through the logistics network. Customers should allow 3–7 business days for the item to reach the warehouse.

## 7. Refund Methods and Timelines

### 7.1 Refund to Credit or Debit Card

Refunds to credit or debit cards are processed within **5–7 business days** of the returned item being received and inspected at the ShopNova warehouse. The time for the refund to appear on the customer's statement depends on their card issuer and may take an additional 1–3 business days.

### 7.2 Refund via Boleto

Boleto payments cannot be reversed directly to the original boleto. Refunds for boleto payments are issued as a bank transfer (TED/DOC) to the customer's nominated bank account. The customer will be asked to provide their bank details (bank name, branch, account number, CPF) when initiating the return. Boleto refunds are processed within **10 business days** of the return being approved.

### 7.3 Refund as Store Credit (Voucher)

Customers who choose store credit will receive a ShopNova voucher code by email within **24 hours** of the return being approved. Store credit has no expiry date and can be used on any future ShopNova purchase. Store credit cannot be converted back to cash.

### 7.4 Refund for Voucher Payments

If the original purchase was made using a ShopNova voucher, the refund will be re-issued as a new voucher of equal value. If the purchase was made using a combination of voucher and card, the voucher portion is refunded as a new voucher and the card portion is refunded to the original card.

## 8. Partial Returns and Bundle Orders

### 8.1 Returning One Item from a Multi-Item Order

Customers may return individual items from a multi-item order without returning the entire order. Each item is assessed independently against the return eligibility criteria. A separate return request must be submitted for each item.

### 8.2 Bundle Discount Clawback

If a multi-item order qualified for a bundle discount (e.g., "Buy 3, get 20% off"), returning one item from the bundle will trigger a discount clawback. The refund amount for the returned item will be the item's full price minus the proportional discount that was applied. The remaining items in the order will retain their discounted price.

For example, if a 3-item bundle totalling R$300 received a 20% discount (saving R$60), each item effectively had R$20 discounted. Returning one item would result in a refund of the item's original price minus R$20.

### 8.3 Gift Returns

Items received as gifts may be returned within the standard return window. The refund will be issued as store credit to the recipient's ShopNova account, not to the original purchaser's payment method, to preserve the gifting experience. The gift recipient must create or log in to a ShopNova account to process a gift return.

## 9. Dispute Resolution

### 9.1 If Your Return is Rejected

If a return request is rejected and the customer believes the rejection is incorrect, they may escalate the case by:

1. Clicking "Dispute Decision" on the return status page within 7 days of the rejection notice.
2. Providing additional evidence (photographs, descriptions, expert opinion if applicable).
3. A senior ShopNova support agent will review the case within 3 business days and issue a final decision.

### 9.2 Consumer Rights

This policy does not limit or waive any rights granted to consumers under the Brazilian Consumer Protection Code (Lei nº 8.078/1990). Customers who believe their statutory rights have been violated may also contact PROCON or other applicable consumer protection bodies.

### 9.3 Escalation to ShopNova Trust Team

For unresolved disputes involving amounts over R$500 or cases of suspected fraud, customers may request escalation to the ShopNova Trust & Safety team. These cases are reviewed within 5 business days and the outcome is final within the ShopNova platform.
