# ShopNova Warranty & Damaged Goods Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2024
**Document Version:** 2.1

---

## 1. Overview and Scope

ShopNova is committed to ensuring that every product sold on the platform meets the quality standards described in the product listing. This Warranty & Damaged Goods Policy explains the protections available to customers when a product arrives damaged, develops a fault after delivery, or fails to perform as described.

ShopNova provides a **platform warranty** that guarantees all products sold are covered by at minimum the terms described in this document. This is separate from any manufacturer warranty that may accompany the product. Where a manufacturer warranty provides broader coverage than ShopNova's platform warranty, the manufacturer warranty takes precedence for the relevant terms.

This policy complies with the Brazilian Consumer Protection Code (CDC), which establishes minimum mandatory warranty periods for durable and non-durable goods. ShopNova's warranty terms meet or exceed these statutory minimums.

### 1.1 Platform Warranty vs Manufacturer Warranty

- **Platform warranty**: Provided and guaranteed by ShopNova. Covers defects reported within the timeframes in this document. Claims are handled through the ShopNova platform.
- **Manufacturer warranty**: Provided by the product's manufacturer. Coverage terms and duration are specified in the warranty card or documentation included with the product. Claims may need to be directed to the manufacturer's authorised service network.

Where a defect is covered by both warranties, ShopNova will coordinate with the manufacturer on the customer's behalf to determine the fastest resolution path.

## 2. Warranty Periods by Product Category

### 2.1 Electronics

Electronics include smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, monitors, TVs, cameras, audio equipment, wearables, and smart home devices.

- **ShopNova platform warranty**: 12 months from the confirmed delivery date.
- **Typical manufacturer warranty**: 12 months (may vary by brand and product).
- **Coverage**: Manufacturing defects, hardware failures, battery defects (where battery capacity drops below 80% of rated capacity within the warranty period under normal usage).
- **Not covered**: Screen cracks, liquid damage, physical damage from drops, unauthorised modifications.

For high-value electronics (smartphones, laptops, cameras) with a purchase price above R$1,000, ShopNova offers an optional **extended warranty** of up to 24 months, purchasable within 30 days of delivery through the ShopNova app.

### 2.2 Home Appliances

Home appliances include refrigerators, washing machines, dryers, dishwashers, microwaves, air conditioners, vacuum cleaners, and similar household electrical equipment.

- **ShopNova platform warranty**: 6 months from the confirmed delivery date.
- **Typical manufacturer warranty**: 12 months (varies by brand; some premium brands offer 24–36 months).
- **Coverage**: Manufacturing defects, motor failures, electrical faults not caused by improper installation or power surges.
- **Not covered**: Damage caused by improper installation, use with incorrect voltage, pest infestation, or failure to follow maintenance instructions.

Customers who experience an appliance fault should first consult the manufacturer's authorised service centre, as many manufacturers provide on-site repair services that are faster than a return.

### 2.3 Furniture

Furniture includes sofas, beds, wardrobes, tables, chairs, shelving units, and outdoor furniture.

- **ShopNova platform warranty**: 24 months from the confirmed delivery date.
- **Coverage**: Structural defects, frame failures, defective mechanisms (recliners, fold-out beds), fabric defects present at time of delivery.
- **Not covered**: Normal wear and tear, fading caused by sunlight exposure, damage from liquids or stains, damage caused during customer assembly if assembly instructions were not followed.

Furniture warranty claims require photographic evidence of the defect. For structural failures, ShopNova may arrange an in-home inspection by a qualified assessor before approving a claim.

### 2.4 Clothing and Footwear

- **ShopNova platform warranty**: 90 days from the confirmed delivery date.
- **Coverage**: Manufacturing defects such as defective stitching that causes seams to separate under normal use, faulty zips or buttons, fabric flaws visible within the first wash.
- **Not covered**: Fading, shrinkage caused by incorrect washing, damage from ironing, normal wear.

Warranty claims for clothing must include a description of the defect and clear photographs. Items must be clean and washed before being submitted for a warranty assessment.

### 2.5 Sports and Leisure Equipment

- **ShopNova platform warranty**: 6 months from the confirmed delivery date for mechanical equipment (bicycles, treadmills, weight benches); 90 days for accessories and non-mechanical items.
- **Coverage**: Frame cracks under normal use, mechanical failures in gears or motors, defective electronic components in equipment.
- **Not covered**: Damage from use outside the product's rated capacity, damage from outdoor exposure without weatherproofing, normal wear on tyres, chains, and moving parts.

### 2.6 Beauty and Personal Care Devices

Electric beauty devices (hair dryers, straighteners, electric shavers, massagers) are covered for **6 months** from delivery against manufacturing defects. Non-electric beauty products (skincare, cosmetics) are not covered by a warranty beyond the right to return if defective on arrival.

### 2.7 Books, Stationery, and Non-Electronic Items

- Physical books with printing defects (missing pages, upside-down printing, binding failures) may be exchanged within **30 days** of delivery.
- Stationery and office supplies with manufacturing defects are covered for **30 days**.

## 3. What Voids the Warranty

The following conditions will void warranty coverage for any product category:

### 3.1 Physical Damage Caused by the Customer

Any physical damage not present at the time of delivery, including but not limited to: cracks, dents, scratches on functional components, broken screens, bent frames, or punctures. ShopNova reserves the right to inspect returned items and decline warranty claims where physical damage is evident.

### 3.2 Liquid or Moisture Damage

Exposure of non-waterproof products to liquids, steam, humidity beyond normal indoor conditions, or submersion. Many electronics contain liquid damage indicators that will be checked upon inspection.

### 3.3 Unauthorised Repairs or Modifications

Any attempt by the customer or an unauthorised third party to open, repair, or modify the product will void the warranty immediately. This includes software modifications (jailbreaking, rooting) on electronic devices.

### 3.4 Incorrect Usage or Installation

Damage resulting from use contrary to the manufacturer's instructions, use with incompatible accessories, installation with incorrect voltage, or use in environmental conditions outside the product's rated specifications.

### 3.5 Normal Wear and Tear

Gradual deterioration consistent with the product's expected lifespan and normal usage patterns. This includes battery capacity degradation below 80% after the warranty period, cosmetic wear on frequently touched surfaces, and thinning of fabric with regular washing.

### 3.6 Third-Party Accessories

Damage caused by the use of non-approved third-party accessories, chargers, or components that are not certified for use with the product.

## 4. Damaged on Arrival (DOA)

### 4.1 Definition

A Damaged on Arrival (DOA) claim applies when a product arrives with visible damage to the product itself or its packaging that is clearly the result of mishandling during transit, rather than a manufacturing defect.

### 4.2 Reporting Window

DOA claims must be reported within **48 hours** of the confirmed delivery date. Claims submitted after 48 hours will not be processed as DOA and will instead be assessed under the standard warranty policy.

### 4.3 How to Report a DOA

1. Before unpacking fully, photograph the outer packaging showing any visible damage.
2. Photograph the inner packaging and the product itself.
3. Go to "My Orders" in the ShopNova app or website.
4. Select the relevant order and click "Report a Problem" → "Item Arrived Damaged."
5. Upload a minimum of three photographs (outer packaging, inner packaging, product).
6. Provide a written description of the damage observed.
7. Submit the report. A support agent will review within 4 hours during business hours.

### 4.4 DOA Resolution

Upon verification of a DOA claim, ShopNova will offer:

- **Immediate replacement**: A new unit shipped within 1 business day (subject to stock availability).
- **Full refund**: Processed within 3 business days to the original payment method.

The customer will not be required to return the damaged item for DOA claims on items valued under R$200. For items valued above R$200, ShopNova will arrange a free pickup of the damaged item.

## 5. How to File a Warranty Claim

### 5.1 Information Required

To file a warranty claim, customers must provide:

- ShopNova order ID
- Description of the fault or defect (when it started, how it manifests, frequency)
- At least two photographs or a short video showing the defect
- Confirmation that the item has not been repaired or modified by a third party
- Confirmation that the damage is not the result of physical misuse

### 5.2 Step-by-Step Claim Process

1. Go to "My Orders" and select the relevant order.
2. Click "Warranty Claim" next to the item.
3. Complete the claim form, providing all required information.
4. Upload supporting photographs or video.
5. Submit the claim. You will receive a claim reference number by email.
6. A ShopNova warranty agent will review the claim within **2 business days**.
7. If additional information is needed, the agent will contact you by email.
8. Once approved, you will be offered a resolution (repair, replacement, or refund).

### 5.3 Response SLA

| Claim type | Initial response | Resolution target |
|---|---|---|
| DOA (damaged on arrival) | 4 hours (business hours) | 1–3 business days |
| Standard warranty claim | 2 business days | 5–10 business days |
| Complex or high-value claim | 3 business days | 10–15 business days |

## 6. Repair, Replacement, or Refund

### 6.1 Decision Framework

When a warranty claim is approved, the resolution is determined as follows:

- **Repair**: Offered first for items that can be restored to full working condition without replacing the unit. Repair is carried out by the manufacturer's authorised service centre or a ShopNova-approved technician.
- **Replacement**: Offered when the item cannot be repaired, or when the repair would take longer than 15 business days. Replacement is with an identical item where available.
- **Refund**: Offered when neither repair nor replacement is possible (item out of stock, discontinued, or repair not viable). Refunded to the original payment method.

### 6.2 Customer Choice Rights

Customers have the right to express a preference for replacement over repair, or refund over replacement. ShopNova will accommodate the customer's preference where operationally possible. If the preferred resolution is not available, ShopNova will explain the reason and offer the next best alternative.

### 6.3 Repair Timeframes

If a repair is approved, the item will be collected by ShopNova's logistics partner. Repairs are typically completed within **10–15 business days**. The customer will be kept updated by email on the repair status. If the repair takes longer than 20 business days, ShopNova will automatically upgrade the resolution to a replacement or refund at the customer's choice.

## 7. Third-Party and Marketplace Sellers

### 7.1 Seller Warranty Obligations

All sellers on the ShopNova marketplace are contractually required to honour this warranty policy. Sellers may offer additional warranty coverage beyond ShopNova's minimum terms, which will be stated in the product listing.

### 7.2 ShopNova's Role as Intermediary

ShopNova acts as the customer's single point of contact for all warranty claims, regardless of whether the fault lies with the manufacturer, the seller, or the carrier. Customers should always file warranty claims through ShopNova and should not contact the seller or manufacturer directly unless advised to do so by ShopNova support.

### 7.3 Seller Non-Compliance

If a seller fails to fulfil a valid warranty claim within the required timeframes, ShopNova will resolve the claim directly at ShopNova's expense and take appropriate action against the seller, which may include suspension from the platform.
