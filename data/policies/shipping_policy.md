# ShopNova Shipping & Delivery Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2024
**Document Version:** 2.8

---

## 1. Overview

ShopNova partners with leading logistics carriers across Brazil to deliver orders to all states and territories. This Shipping & Delivery Policy describes the shipping methods available, estimated delivery timeframes, freight costs, and the procedures that apply when deliveries are delayed, failed, or lost.

All delivery estimates are provided in business days unless stated otherwise. Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding national Brazilian public holidays. Estimates begin from the date the seller dispatches the order, not the date of purchase.

ShopNova uses a network of third-party carriers including Correios, Jadlog, Total Express, Azul Cargo, and Loggi. The carrier assigned to each order is determined automatically based on the customer's location, package dimensions, and the seller's logistics setup. Customers will be notified of the assigned carrier and tracking number by email once the order is dispatched.

## 2. Shipping Methods

### 2.1 Standard Shipping

Standard shipping is available for all orders delivered within Brazil. Estimated delivery time is **5–8 business days** from the dispatch date, depending on the destination state.

Standard shipping is the default option at checkout. The cost is calculated based on the package weight, dimensions, and delivery address, and is displayed before payment is confirmed.

### 2.2 Express Shipping

Express shipping delivers orders in **1–2 business days** from the dispatch date. Express shipping is available for orders meeting the following criteria:

- Order total of R$50 or more (after discounts).
- Delivery address in a capital city or major metropolitan area.
- Order placed before 12:00 noon (Brasília time) on a business day.
- Product weight under 10kg and dimensions within carrier express limits.

Express shipping is not available for all product categories. Large furniture, appliances, and hazardous goods are excluded from express shipping regardless of order value.

### 2.3 Same-Day Delivery

Same-day delivery is available in select cities (currently São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Belo Horizonte) for orders placed before 10:00 am on business days. Same-day delivery is subject to seller participation — not all sellers on the ShopNova platform offer this service. Availability is shown at checkout.

### 2.4 Scheduled Delivery

For large items such as furniture and appliances, ShopNova offers scheduled delivery. Customers select a preferred delivery date and time slot (morning or afternoon) during checkout. Scheduled delivery is available Tuesday through Saturday in most regions. A confirmation message will be sent 24 hours before the delivery date.

## 4. Freight Costs and Free Shipping

### 4.1 Freight Cost Calculation

Freight costs are calculated at checkout based on:

- Package weight (actual weight or volumetric weight, whichever is greater)
- Package dimensions
- Delivery address (zip code)
- Selected shipping method (standard, express, scheduled)

The exact freight cost is displayed on the checkout page before payment is confirmed. ShopNova does not add any markup to carrier rates.

### 4.2 Free Shipping Threshold

Orders with a product total of **R$75 or more** (after discounts, before freight) qualify for free standard shipping to any address in Brazil. Free shipping applies automatically at checkout when the threshold is met.

The following categories are excluded from the free shipping promotion:

- Furniture and large home décor items
- Home appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners)
- Large sports equipment (bicycles, treadmills, weight benches)
- Hazardous goods

### 4.3 Promotional Free Shipping

ShopNova periodically offers promotional free shipping with no minimum order value. These promotions are communicated via the ShopNova app, email newsletter, and the promotional banner on the homepage. Promotional free shipping has an expiry date and may be limited to specific product categories or sellers.

## 5. Order Tracking

### 5.1 How to Track Your Order

Once the seller dispatches the order, ShopNova sends a tracking notification by email containing:

- The assigned carrier name
- The tracking number
- A direct link to the carrier's tracking page

Tracking can also be accessed at any time through "My Orders" in the ShopNova app or website. Click on the relevant order to see the current delivery status and estimated delivery date.

### 5.2 When Tracking Activates

Tracking information typically activates within **4–8 hours** of the seller handing the package to the carrier. In some cases, particularly with Correios, tracking may take up to 24 hours to update after dispatch. If no tracking movement is shown after 48 hours from the dispatch notification, customers should contact ShopNova support.

### 5.3 Tracking Status Descriptions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Order dispatched | Seller has handed the package to the carrier |
| In transit | Package is moving through the carrier network |
| Out for delivery | Package is on the delivery vehicle and will arrive today |
| Delivery attempted | Carrier attempted delivery but no one was available |
| Held at post office | Package is awaiting collection at a local post office |
| Delivered | Carrier has confirmed delivery |

## 6. Late and Failed Deliveries

### 6.1 What Counts as a Late Delivery

A delivery is considered late when the package has not been delivered by the end of the estimated delivery window shown at checkout. The estimated delivery date is displayed on the order confirmation email and in "My Orders."

### 6.2 Late Delivery Compensation

If an order is delivered **5 or more business days** after the estimated delivery date, and the delay is caused by the carrier or ShopNova (not by the customer), the customer is entitled to a **10% discount voucher** on their next ShopNova purchase. The voucher is valid for 90 days from the date of issue.

To claim late delivery compensation:

1. Go to "My Orders" and select the delayed order.
2. Click "Report Late Delivery."
3. Confirm the delay. ShopNova will verify against carrier data.
4. If confirmed, the voucher will be issued within 1 business day.

### 6.3 Delays Due to Force Majeure

ShopNova will not issue compensation for delays caused by events outside its control, including natural disasters, extreme weather, strikes, civil unrest, or acts of government. In such cases, ShopNova will communicate proactively with affected customers and provide updated delivery estimates.

## 7. Lost Packages

### 7.1 When to Open a Lost Package Claim

A package is considered potentially lost if it has not been delivered within **15 calendar days** past the estimated delivery date and the tracking has not updated in 7 or more days. Customers should not open a lost package claim before this threshold, as packages in transit sometimes go without tracking updates.

### 7.2 How to Open a Claim

1. Go to "My Orders" and select the order.
2. Click "Report Missing Package."
3. Confirm that the tracking has not updated in 7+ days.
4. ShopNova will open an investigation with the carrier within 1 business day.

### 7.3 Investigation Timeline

Carrier investigations typically take **5–10 business days**. During the investigation, ShopNova will provide updates every 3 business days. If the package is confirmed lost, ShopNova will offer the customer a choice of:

- Full replacement shipment at no cost (subject to stock availability)
- Full refund to the original payment method

ShopNova processes the replacement or refund within 2 business days of the investigation conclusion.

## 8. Address and Delivery Issues

### 8.1 Incorrect Address Entered by Customer

If a customer enters an incorrect delivery address at checkout, they must contact ShopNova support immediately. Address corrections are only possible before the seller dispatches the order. Once dispatched, the delivery address cannot be changed. ShopNova is not responsible for failed or lost deliveries resulting from an incorrect address provided by the customer.

### 8.2 Failed Delivery Attempts

If no one is available to receive the delivery, the carrier will make up to **3 delivery attempts** on consecutive business days. After the third failed attempt, the package will be held at the nearest post office or carrier depot for **7 calendar days**.

Customers will be notified by email and SMS after each failed attempt, with instructions for rescheduling or collecting the package.

### 8.3 Package Held at Post Office

If a package is being held at the post office, the customer must collect it within 7 calendar days of the hold notification. The customer must bring a valid photo ID and the tracking number. If the package is not collected within the 7-day window, it will be returned to the seller and the customer will receive a refund minus the return freight cost.

## 9. Large and Heavy Items

### 9.1 Special Handling for Furniture and Appliances

Large items including furniture, home appliances, gym equipment, and items weighing over 30kg are subject to special delivery conditions:

- Delivery is to the **ground floor or building entrance only**, unless a room-of-choice delivery upgrade is selected at checkout.
- Scheduled delivery slots are available Tuesday through Saturday.
- Customers must ensure someone is present during the delivery window.
- Carriers will call the customer 30–60 minutes before arrival.

### 9.2 Room-of-Choice Delivery

For an additional fee, ShopNova offers room-of-choice delivery for large items in select cities. The delivery team will bring the item to the specified room on any floor. This service is available at checkout for eligible products and addresses.

### 9.3 Assembly Services

ShopNova offers a paid assembly add-on for furniture and large appliances in major cities. The assembly team arrives on the same day as the delivery and completes installation within the scheduled time slot. Assembly services must be booked at checkout and cannot be added after the order is placed.
